Appropriately Showing Respect to Veterans in your Community

Guidelines for honoring those who served while respecting their individuality and humanity

DON'T: Make a Scene

Don't call attention to a veteran's status to others in media or another circumstance unless given explicit permission. [3]

Many veterans prefer privacy regarding their service and may feel uncomfortable when publicly identified without consent.

DO: Remember They're Human

A veteran isn't just a veteran, they are a human being with feelings and have experiences different to that of civilians. [3]

Engage with veterans as complete individuals with unique personalities, interests, and needs beyond their military service.

DON'T: Stereotype

Not all veterans have PTSD or are war heroes [1]. But all deserve our kindness.

Avoid making assumptions about a veteran's political views, experiences, or mental health based solely on their veteran status.
